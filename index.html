What is an app?

An app is a self-contained pieces of software.

Everybody knows what an app is, like the ones on the app stores. A proper definition would be a self contained program that is dedicated to a specific task. There are about 5 types of apps. These are categorized as web apps, native apps, hybrid apps, killer apps, and legacy apps
